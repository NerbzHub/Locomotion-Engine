⚔ DUNGEON DEFENSE ⚔
a whimsical pixel tower defence
HOW TO PLAY
⬩ Monsters march down the dungeon path toward your gate.
⬩ Tap a tile next to the path to build a tower.
⬩ Towers auto-attack enemies in range.
⬩ Kill enemies for gold. Spend gold on more towers or upgrades.
⬩ Tap an existing tower to upgrade or sell it.
⬩ Survive all waves. Each wave is tougher, but pays more gold!
